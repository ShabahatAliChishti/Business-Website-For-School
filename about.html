About us

Our School

Metropolitan Academy, since its existence in 1967 has covered a long journey of excellence in a pragmatic way. The management is committed to serve the society with the motive to provide quality education to the wards of the marooned masses to alleviate the lengthening shadows of illiteracy in the society at compatible fee structure with the inhabitants income . The management has accelerated its momentum with a complete focus on quality, standard, and knowledge dissemination process. The Academy has developed distinctive culture which is intended to focus on the satisfaction of a child’s social, physical and emotional needs and wants. Initially the Academy was grooming the Metric stream students but on the compelling and repeated requests of parents, a unique opportunity of imparting international level education (O’ level) at the most affordable fee structure is also available.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create happy, balanced, caring and professional environment in which peoples thrive and meet their full potential. We endeavor earnestly that all stakeholders benefit maximal through quality education as per challenges of 21st century with equal emphasis on personality development of our students.

The school envisages provision of quality education to its student on most modern lines, commensurate with national/international aspiration and present day technological demands. We tend to provide an atmosphere conductive to child’s healthy mental and physical development. Condensely we tend to be

“The pace in the field of O’level and Metric stream education through achieving excellence and human resource development”
